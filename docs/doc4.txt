Transformers are deep learning models designed for handling sequential data, especially in natural language processing. 
They use a mechanism called self-attention to understand relationships between words in a sentence.

Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT, BERT, and Qwen are based on transformer architecture. 
These models are trained on massive datasets and can perform tasks such as text generation, summarization, translation, and question answering.

Transformers have replaced older models like RNNs and LSTMs due to their ability to process data in parallel and capture long-range dependencies.
